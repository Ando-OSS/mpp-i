# Code Of Conduct

MPP-I discussions should stay technical, respectful, and useful.

Participants should:

- focus criticism on ideas, text, protocol behaviour, and evidence,
- assume good faith unless behaviour proves otherwise,
- avoid personal attacks,
- keep commercial or promotional posts relevant to MPP-I,
- disclose conflicts of interest when proposing ecosystem, vendor, or settlement-method changes.

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments that are abusive, spammy, off-topic, or disruptive.

# Q&A

## Is MPP-I a coin?

No. MPP-I is not a coin, token, or settlement asset. It is a proposed payment profile for metered inference inside the MPP family.

## Does MPP-I replace MPP?

No. MPP-I is designed to be compatible with MPP's outer payment flow while adding inference-specific lifecycle semantics.

## Does MPP-I require x402?

No. MPP-I can be designed to work with x402-compatible settlement paths where they preserve run binding, cumulative authorisation, and receipt semantics.

## Does the payment plane see prompts or outputs?

No. The payment/control plane should not see raw prompt or raw output content. It carries payment state, meter frames, low-credit events, drain events, settlement references, and receipts.

## What happens if vouchers stop?

The provider admits no new billable interval, drains only through already authorised headroom, stops at the disclosed boundary, settles the metered amount due, and emits a final receipt.

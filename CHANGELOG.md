# Changelog

## Paper v1.0 - 2026-06-28

- Initial public MPP-I paper.
- Defines MPP-I as an MPP-compatible, inference-native payment profile for metered inference.
- Includes the proposed inference payment lifecycle, run-scoped authorisation, cumulative credit grants, meter frames, low-credit and drain behaviour, stop semantics, settlement references, and final receipts.
